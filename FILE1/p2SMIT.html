SAYLANI SMIT

Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT): It appears to be a program offered by the Saylani Welfare International Trust, a non-profit organization focusing on social welfare in Pakistan, particularly Karachi. Focus: SMIT aims to equip young people with computer programming skills to prepare them for careers in the information technology sector. Programs: They offer courses in various IT domains like web and mobile application development, CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) certification, graphic designing, and computer-based accounting. Goal: SMIT strives to develop a large pool of skilled software developers in Pakistan, potentially contributing to the country's economic growth

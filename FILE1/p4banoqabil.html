BANO QABIL

Bano Qabil is a youth education initiative launched in June 2022 by the Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan. Goal: Empower and equip young people in Pakistan with advanced IT skills. Increase their employability and open doors for careers and freelancing opportunities in the IT sector. Contribute to the development of a skilled workforce in the Pakistani IT industry. Target Audience: Bano Qabil primarily targets young people in Pakistan seeking to build their IT skills. Course Offerings: The program offers 100% free, advanced, in-demand, and practical IT courses. Benefits: Participants gain valuable IT skills and knowledge to compete in the job market. The program aims to facilitate job placements for up to 90% of students after course completion. Bano Qabil prepares individuals for self-employment through freelancing platforms like Upwork and Fiverr. It empowers women facing economic challenges by equipping them with relevant IT skills.

    The specific course list might vary depending on location and demand, but some possible options include:
  1. Web development Front End
  2. Web development Back End
  3. App development (web or mobile)
  4. Digital marketing
  5. Graphic design
  6. Freelancing and Amazon FBA Virtual Assistant skills
